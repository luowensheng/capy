  <article class="card">
    <h1>Lemon olive oil cake</h1>
    <div class="meta">
      <span>Serves 8</span>
      <span>45 minutes</span>
    </div>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul class="ingredients">
      <li><span class="qty">1 1/2 cups</span> <span class="name">all-purpose flour</span></li>
      <li><span class="qty">3/4 cup</span> <span class="name">olive oil</span></li>
      <li><span class="qty">1 cup</span> <span class="name">sugar</span></li>
      <li><span class="qty">3</span> <span class="name">eggs</span></li>
      <li><span class="qty">2 tablespoons</span> <span class="name">lemon zest</span></li>
      <li><span class="qty">1/4 cup</span> <span class="name">lemon juice</span></li>
      <li><span class="qty">1 teaspoon</span> <span class="name">baking powder</span></li>
      <li><span class="qty">1/4 teaspoon</span> <span class="name">salt</span></li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Method</h2>
    <ol class="steps">
      <li>Preheat oven to 350F and grease a 9-inch round pan.</li>
      <li>Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.</li>
      <li>In another bowl, beat sugar and eggs until light.</li>
      <li>Stir in olive oil, lemon zest, and lemon juice.</li>
      <li>Fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture.</li>
      <li>Pour into the pan and bake for 35-40 minutes.</li>
      <li>Cool on a rack before slicing.</li>
    </ol>
    <div class="tip"><strong>Tip:</strong> For extra zing, glaze with powdered sugar and lemon juice.</div>
